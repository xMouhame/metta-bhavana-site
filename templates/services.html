Therapy Services Offered

Metta Bhavana Psychological & Spiritual Wellness provides compassionate, culturally mindful, evidence-based therapy for individuals, couples, and families. Below is a list of the issues and areas in which support is offered.

  • Depression
  • Trauma

  • Attachment Issues
  • Burnout

  • Panic Disorders
  • Physical Wellness

  • Relationship Issues
  • Spirituality and Religion
  • Life Transitions
  • Stress

Therapeutic Services

Individual Counseling

One-on-one therapy focused on personal growth, emotional well-being, and healing. Sessions may include CBT, mindfulness, ACT, DBT skills, and other evidence-based approaches.

Marital Counseling

Support for couples aiming to improve communication, rebuild trust, deepen connection, and navigate relationship challenges with compassion.

Family Counseling

A safe, structured environment to improve family communication, reduce conflict, and strengthen relationships among family members.
