About Papa Fall

Papa earned his Bachelor’s degree from the University of Wisconsin–Oshkosh and a Master of Social Work (MSW) from the University of Wisconsin–Madison with a specialization in Mental Health.

He has extensive experience providing individual, family, and group therapy in both outpatient and inpatient settings. Papa is trained in CBT, DBT, ACT, Motivational Interviewing, Mindfulness, and other evidence-based approaches.

His work focuses on mental health assessment, trauma, cultural competency, mindfulness, burnout, and substance use support. He also serves as a lecturer at UW-Madison and Madison College.

Outside of clinical work, Papa enjoys traveling, reading, exercising, dancing, and spending time outdoors.
